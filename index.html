About PageForward

We are a group of passionate students driven by the vision of a more educated and sustainable world. At PageForward, we collect books, notebooks, papers, and other educational materials, giving them a second life and a second chance to make a difference.

Our mission extends beyond just recycling - we're creating a movement of conscious education that respects both learning and our environment.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has access to quality education materials while promoting environmental sustainability through the reuse and recycling of educational resources.

Our Mission

To collect, redistribute, and recycle educational materials, making them accessible to underprivileged students while reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in education.
